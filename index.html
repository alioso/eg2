    Led art Festa

    A wonderland of light, art, and kinetic experience, it takes place on an enchanting island in South Korea. Headlined by Bruce Munro, an artist best known for immersive large-scale light-based installations inspired by his interest in shared human experience, and 20 other international leading light artists, the 19-acre former tea museum site will be transformed into an indoor and open-air festival, an extraordinary experience that expands the imagination and sensory of visitors. Once it opens its door in 2018, over 4000 visitors per year are anticipated and plans are underway to bring the festival to Qatar, Morocco, and China.
